<p>Dear Parents and Guardians,</p>
<p>Welcome to our E-Open House at Yuhua Primary School!</p>
<p>We are delighted to have you join us on this virtual journey as you explore
what makes Yuhua Primary School a warm, vibrant and nurturing environment
for your child. Through this online platform, we will be showcasing our
school facilities, school programmes and some videos from our Yuhualites.
We hope this journey gives you valuable insights into school life at Yuhua.</p>

<h4><strong>Helping Your Child Transition to Primary One</strong></h4>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Our Peer Support Programme</strong>
<br>Is your child feeling anxious about starting Primary One? At Yuhua, we
understand and that’s why we create a caring and inclusive environment
where every child feels supported. Through our Peer Support Programme,
students look out for one another and build lasting friendships that help
them settle in with confidence.</p>
<p>Let our Yuhualites share their stories and reassure your child that they
are not alone.</p>
